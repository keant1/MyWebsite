About Me

I’m an undergraduate student studying Physics and Management at McMaster University. My passions include science & technology, innovation, community-building and sharing ingenuity.

Projects

PID Control

PID Control of a Simulated Thermal Electric Cooler using between a PC and a MSP430 Microcontroller. PID Control algorithms were executed on MatLab while the thermal system response was executed through the microcontroller using C.

Tools: C, MatLab, MSP430 Microcontroller, UART Communications Protocol

Ultrasonic Range Finder

Electronics design to provide a digital readout of the distance of an object from the measuring device using ultrasonic acoustic waves and electronic components.

Tools:Oscilloscope, Multimeter, Bench Power Supply, Op-Amps 555 Timers, Digital and Analog Circuit Components

Sequential Logic Design

Circuit design to provide a digital readout through a 7-segment display of my student number to cycle through my student number using Boolean logic, digital and analog circuitry.

Tools: Breadboards, Logic Gates, Function Generators, Oscilloscope, Multimeter, Bench Power Supply

Engine Controls Strategy

Researched and developed the control strategy to command torque from the internal combustion engine (ICE). The ICE Control ring determines the engine state and sends torque commands based on the active energy management strategy in the team’s custom-made hybrid supervisory controller. The team’s HSC optimizes fuel and battery consumption, balancing rear high voltage electric motor and engine use.

Diagnostics Specialist

Propulsion Modelling and Controls (PCM) sub team member responsible for liaising with the Systems Safety sub-team to develop algorithms for fault detection and implement strategies for remedial action.

System Functional Interface Analysis

PCM participant in the Propulsion Controls and Modelling & Connected Automated Vehicles interface analysis which identified hazards and their system level impact to determine system safety requirements for implementation.
